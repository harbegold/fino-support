FAQ

  • How do I reset my password?
    On the sign-in screen, tap "Sign Up" to create a new account, or contact us to reset your existing password.
  • How do hearts work?
    You start with 5 hearts. Each wrong answer costs 1 heart. Hearts regenerate 1 every 4 hours, or you can refill them in the Shop. Pro users get unlimited hearts.
  • How do I cancel my subscription?
    Go to Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions on your iPhone and select Fino to manage or cancel your subscription. You'll keep Pro access until the end of your billing period.
  • How do streaks work?
    Complete at least one lesson per day to build your streak. Miss a day and it resets — unless you have a Streak Freeze (buy one in the Shop for 100 coins).
  • What's included in Fino Pro?
    Unlimited hearts, all 9 courses (including Insurance, Real Estate & Retirement), streak shield, and unlimited daily lessons. Start with a free 3-day trial.
  • Is my data safe?
    Yes. Your data is stored securely and we never sell your personal information to third parties.
